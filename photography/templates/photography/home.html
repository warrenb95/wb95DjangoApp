Who am I?

Hi, I'm Warren and I am a Computer Science(BSc) student studying at the university of Brighton. Photography is a fun, creative outlet for me. I'm always sat at my desk or working on my laptop at university and so I use photography as an escape from the screen. Staring at lines of code all day can give the best of people a headache so it's nice to just be able to get up and do something else fun yet productive.

My images haven't got a shared theme or anything special like that. I simply just make images of things that interest me in the moment. Although recently I have tried to focus on the creation of image stories/collections. This has been fun because it's getting me to think more about the images I'm making and how they all connect with each other to form a story.
If you are interested on what I am offering please feel free to contact me here.
